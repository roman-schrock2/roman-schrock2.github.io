Glitch Art


In this project, we were asked to take existing images and cause glitches to occur using alternative programs. â€‹Glitch art is the purposeful corruption of either digital or analog data that alters the image in a meaningful way. For this project, I took images and converted them into RAW files using photoshop. Then using programs that are not intended for photos (Notepad++, Audacity, etc.) I altered the data to give me my final products.

On this project, my intention was to take existing art works that were made using traditional mediums and bring them into the technological era by corrupting them, causing them to glitch.
