Final Project


For the final project, we got to create a piece of digital media art using any method we had covered throught the semester. I got inspired by one of my other projects and wanted to make something that included 3D modeling and rendering. Using this created entity, I then would create a mashup as we did using Photoshop.


The year is 2150 and all life on earth is gone. The only remaining living organism is this plant which has been carefully preserved. In the background is a futuristic society that has collapsed and is now in ruin. The small sapling is sitting within inset domes colored to symbolize the earth with rings holding the sun and the moon surrounding it. In this piece, I intended to show that nature and life is the center of the world and we must preserve it at all cost.
