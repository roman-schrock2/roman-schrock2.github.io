3D Art


Digital Media Art expands past the computer screen. This project showed us how what we make in digital worlds can be translated to the real world using 3D printing technology. In this project, we created statues in the game Minecraft then exported them and printed them. I created a character from the game Among Us and printed the final to be about an inch tall.
